Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think and learn like humans.

Machine learning is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Deep learning is a type of machine learning using neural networks with many layers, capable of processing large amounts of unstructured data.

Natural language processing (NLP) is a branch of AI that helps computers understand, interpret and manipulate human language.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines large language models with information retrieval systems to provide accurate, up-to-date responses grounded in external knowledge.
